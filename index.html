sonikl records.

null and subtle.

a lo-fi, slow-paced, independent fashion label launched in 2017 in conjunction with borneo fashion week 2017. brand identity; garment design and production; visual communications; web design, development and management and social media management by shuz m.

://instagram.com/nullandsubtle.co

seamless society.

an independent knowledge-sharing platform by shuz m. focusing on fashion and tailoring related workshops including 'keep calm and sew' workshops in collaboration with outdated, publika (2012); basic pattern drafting and tailoring workshop for zvsh studio (2011-2012), basic sewing workshop in collaboration with sewist nest, UNHCR and fugee school, gombak (2015); basic beadwork workshop in collaboration with jelujur and hari hasanah (2016); and the ongoing tailoring hack workshops (2015-present).

://shuzmordiffi.com/seamlesssociety.html
://instagram.com/seamlesssociety
