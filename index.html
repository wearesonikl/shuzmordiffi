bio.

shuz mordiffi is a multi-disciplinary designer and founder of SONIKL STUDIO, an alternative community-centric creative studio based in kuala lumpur, malaysia.

a strong believer of the d-i-y ethos, shuz began her journey in 2007 by co-founding Malaysia’s pioneer online blogshop, THE SHOPLIFTERS. after graduating from UiTM in ART & DESIGN in 2010, she began her professional career with ZVSH STUDIO, a one woman fashion studio, and gained 11 years experience in garment design, branding, graphic design, web design, e-commerce management and brand management.

2017 saw shuz organising CYBERJAYA MUSIC FESTIVAL, followed by CYBERJAYA MULTIMEDIA FESTIVAL in 2018. sonikl studio was founded as a creative ecosystem, and has since pivoted into a creative studio designed for self-starters and the d-i-y community.
